SECTION 2: MEDPAY — YOUR MOST IMPORTANT
COVERAGE RIGHT NOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════

What it covers:
Your MedPay covers up to $10,000 in
medical expenses for you and any
passengers in your vehicle, regardless
of who caused the accident.

What MedPay pays for:
- Emergency room visits
- Ambulance fees
- Hospital stays
- Surgery
- X-rays, MRIs, CT scans
- Physical therapy
- Dental treatment from accident
- Prosthetic devices
- Funeral expenses (up to policy limit)

How to file your MedPay claim:
1. Call Progressive: 1-800-776-4737
2. Say: "I need to file a MedPay claim
   under my policy number TX-2847-JAK-2024"
3. Provide: date of accident,
   description, police report number
4. Submit medical bills directly to
   Progressive as they arrive
5. Progressive pays provider directly
   OR reimburses you

Critical MedPay rules:
- File within 2 years of accident date
- Does NOT increase your rates
  (MedPay is no-fault coverage)
- Does NOT require you to prove
  other driver's fault
- Pays FIRST — before health insurance,
  before other driver's liability
- If you recover from other driver later,
  Progressive may seek subrogation
  (reimbursement) for what they paid

What to say when you call:
"Hi, I was in an accident on [date]
and I need to file a MedPay claim.
My policy number is TX-2847-JAK-2024.
I have medical bills I need covered
while the liability claim is pending."

SECTION 3: COLLISION COVERAGE —
YOUR CAR GETS FIXED NOW
═══════════════════════════════════════════

What it covers:
Damage to YOUR vehicle from the
collision, regardless of fault.
Your deductible: $500

How it works:
1. You pay $500 deductible
2. Progressive pays repair costs above $500
3. If other driver is at fault:
   Progressive recovers your $500
   deductible from their insurer
   (called subrogation — you get it back)
4. Your car is repaired now, not after
   months of liability dispute

When to use collision vs other driver's liability:
USE YOUR COLLISION when:
- You need car fixed immediately
- Other driver's insurer is delaying
- Liability is disputed
- Other driver has no insurance

USE OTHER DRIVER'S LIABILITY when:
- Clear liability, cooperative insurer
- You can wait 2-4 weeks
- You want to avoid deductible

For rear-end accidents in Texas:
Use collision immediately —
Progressive repairs your car within
5-7 business days at a Progressive
Direct Repair shop.
Call: 1-800-776-4737
Or file online: progressive.com/claims

Total loss rules:
If repair cost exceeds 75% of car value,
Progressive declares total loss.
Payment = Actual Cash Value (ACV)
of vehicle at time of accident.
ACV based on: year, make, model,
mileage, condition, local market prices.

What to say:
"I need to file a collision claim.
My vehicle was rear-ended on [date].
I have a police report number [X].
I'd like to take it to a Progressive
Direct Repair shop."

SECTION 4: UNINSURED/UNDERINSURED
MOTORIST — YOUR SAFETY NET
═══════════════════════════════════════════

Your UM/UIM limits: $100,000 per person
                    $300,000 per accident

When UM applies (Uninsured Motorist):
- Other driver has NO insurance
- Hit and run — driver fled
- Other driver's insurer denies claim
- Stolen vehicle caused accident

When UIM applies (Underinsured Motorist):
- Other driver HAS insurance BUT
- Their limits are too low for your damages
- Example: They have $30K minimum Texas
  but your medical bills are $80K
- Your UIM covers the $50K gap

How to file UM/UIM claim:
1. Must have police report (required in TX)
2. Call Progressive: 1-800-776-4737
3. Say: "I need to file a UM claim —
   the other driver had no insurance /
   fled the scene"
4. Progressive investigates and pays
   up to your $100,000 limit

UM stacking in Texas:
Texas allows stacking UM coverage
across multiple vehicles on same policy.
If you have 2 cars insured:
$100K × 2 = $200K effective UM coverage.
Check your declarations page for
stacking availability.

Critical UM rules:
- Must report hit and run to police
  within 24 hours
- Must file UM claim within 2 years
- Cannot settle with other driver
  without Progressive's consent
- Progressive has right to subrogate
  against other driver after paying you

What to say for hit and run:
"I need to file a UM claim. I was
involved in a hit and run accident
on [date] in [city]. I have a police
report number [X]. The other driver
fled and I was not able to get their
information."

SECTION 8: WHAT TO DO IN FIRST
24 HOURS — YOUR POLICY CHECKLIST
═══════════════════════════════════════════

□ IMMEDIATELY at scene:
  - Call 911 (required for injury in TX)
  - Get police report number
  - Photograph all damage
  - Get other driver: name, license,
    plate, insurance card, phone
  - Get witnesses: name and phone
  - Do NOT say "I'm fine" or "my fault"

□ WITHIN 2 HOURS:
  - Call Progressive: 1-800-776-4737
  - Report accident (required by policy)
  - File MedPay claim if injured
  - Request rental car activation

□ WITHIN 24 HOURS:
  - See doctor or urgent care
    (MedPay covers it, no cost to you)
  - File collision claim if car damaged
  - Request roadside if car undriveable
  - Send UM claim if hit and run

□ WITHIN 1 WEEK:
  - Follow up with doctor
  - Keep ALL medical bills and receipts
  - Document lost wages
  - Do NOT give recorded statement
    to other driver's insurer
  - Consult personal injury attorney
    if injuries are significant

□ DO NOT:
  - Accept any settlement before
    completing medical treatment
  - Give recorded statement to
    other driver's insurer
  - Post about accident on social media
  - Sign any releases
  - Dispose of damaged vehicle parts

SECTION 10: SUBROGATION —
GETTING YOUR DEDUCTIBLE BACK
═══════════════════════════════════════════

What subrogation means:
After Progressive pays your claim,
they pursue the at-fault driver's
insurer to recover what they paid.
When they recover, they return
YOUR deductible to you.

Timeline:
- Typically 3-6 months after claim
- Automatic — you don't need to
  do anything
- Progressive sends you a check
  for your $500 deductible when
  subrogation is successful

When subrogation may fail:
- Other driver is uninsured
  (your UM coverage handles this)
- Disputed liability
- Other driver has no assets
